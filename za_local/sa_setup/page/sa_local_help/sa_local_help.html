{{ __("VAT 201 Flow") }}

Create a VAT201 Return, set the company and period dates, then use Get VAT Transactions to build the working paper from invoices and classified journal entries. Review the linked transactions, reconcile the totals against the VAT Audit Report, export the summary and linked transactions, and file manually through SARS eFiling.

{{ __("Sales Invoice Tax Invoice Checks") }}

On South-Africa-only sites, the setup wizard sets the SA print formats as site defaults. Use the Check SA Tax Invoice action on Sales Invoice to confirm threshold guidance: no dedicated tax invoice at or below R50, abridged tax invoice up to R5,000, and full tax invoice above R5,000. Confirm company VAT numbers, addresses, item descriptions, quantities, and customer tax details where required before issuing live documents.
